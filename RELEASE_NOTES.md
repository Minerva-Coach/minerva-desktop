**What's new in this release:**

- Connections moved to the ⓘ About panel — platform and calendar links are now in the About modal instead of the main panel. A pulsing blue dot on ⓘ and a banner guide you there until you've visited it once.

- No calendar warning — a compact amber warning now appears in the panel if no calendar is linked, with a quick link to fix it in About.

- "What is this window?" intro on the Minerva Core Behavior Counts overlay — behavior counts are dimmed until you acknowledge the intro. Tap "More info" to open the icon key.

- Meeting Platforms — the connections section is now labelled "Meeting Platforms" instead of "Accounts".

- Fixed: What's New popup now appears correctly after an auto-update.
