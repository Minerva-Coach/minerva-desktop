**What's new in this release:**

- Core Meeting Skills — real-time skill coaching has moved into the main panel. The separate floating counts window is gone; a small "Core Meeting Skills" box now tracks your progress and flashes green when you use a skill, red when it's a good moment to use one, and yellow (with the relevant icon) for warnings like filler words, speaking pace, or talk time.

- Intent Alignment tracking is temporarily paused while we improve its accuracy — the other three core skills (Open-Ended Questions, Exploring Emotions or Values, Summarizing Statement) are unaffected.

- Fixed: the "My Meetings page" link in the post-meeting popup now opens the right page instead of a 404.
